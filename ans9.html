1. BOLD TAG
    • it is used to bold any tag
    • it only highlight the text but does not provide any importance.
  2. STRONG TAG
    • it also bold the tag just like a bold tag but it give weightage tothe word
    • it is used for text reader to let that know the specific word in para or on site have a specific meaning or importance.
  3. STRIKETHROUGH TAG
    • it is used to strike through a word or cut any word by a line from the midle
  4. DEL TAG
    • it is used to show a del on any product on site
    • the output of the tag is just like a strikethrough
  5. UNDERLINE TAG
    • it is used to underline a word
